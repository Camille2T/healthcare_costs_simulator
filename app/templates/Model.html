Acute Care hospitals have been split into four categories depending on the availibility of data:
- the inpatient Medicare dataset which discloses the average charges at the DRG level.
- the average spending per Medicare patient dataset

In all four models, the following variables have been used:
- the diagnostic (DRG),
- the Hospital Reference Region (HRR),
- hospital ownership,
- the average aggregated gross income and the average wage at the county level,
- some demographic variables: the percentage of over 65 year old people, the percentage of African Americans and the percentage of Hispanics.
