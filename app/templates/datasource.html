The following data is used on this simulator.

Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services:

- Medicare Provider Utilization and Payment Data: Inpatient dataset 2017 (most recent available): provides information on inpatient discharges for Medicare fee-for-service beneficiaries. It includes information on utilization, payment (total payment and Medicare payment), and hospital-specific charges for the more than 3,000 U.S. hospitals

- Hospital General Information is a list of all hospitals that have been registered with Medicare. The list includes addresses, hospital ownership, and overall hospital rating.

- Hospital spending per beneficiary : this index shows whether Medicare spends more, less, or about the same for an episode of care at a specific hospital compared to all hospitals nationally.


Local data (at county level):

- IRS: statistics of income county data 2017

- United States Census Bureau: County Population by Characteristics 2017

- OpenDataSoft: US Zip Code Latitude and Longitude

- The Darthmouth institute for Health Policy & clinical practice: ZIP code to HRR crosswalk file captures the hospital referral regions (HRR).
